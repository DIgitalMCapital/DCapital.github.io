About Us

DIGITAL MISSION CAPITAL helping founders win.

On-Demand SaaS Receivable discount trade finance to grow your business from $10k - $1m

Digital Mission Capital provides a clear, non-dilutive alternative for founders choosing how to grow their company. By providing a cash advance equal to the yearly price of the software subscription, a company that uses DMC can have access to the full annual value of a customer subscription right away. Stated in fewer terms, startups using DMC can immediately turn their monthly recurring revenue (MRR) into annual recurring revenue (ARR). The MRR to ARR conversion allows for companies to focus on acquiring more customers and invest in the critical infrastructure needed for expansion. DMC’s charged fees are comparable to current competitors in the financing space. The key to DMC’s financing is its integration into its customers’ accounting, billing, and subscription management systems. The information from these systems is used to instantly decide whether the company is qualified to receive financing from DMC. The founders claim that customers can turn their MRR to ARR “within hours of applying.”

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is DMC?

    DMC is the new way for SaaS companies to finance growth without equity dilution or venture debt. We give you On-demand access to cash for the full annual value of your monthly and quarterly subscriptions.

  • How does DMC work?

    DMC syncs with your existing subscription management, accounting, and banking systems to underwrite your business.

  • What type of funding does DMC offer?

    DMC offers investment capital with offers ranging from $10k to $1m through a SaaS receivable discount trade finance.we don’t take equity, personal guarantees, warrants, or hidden fees. The amount accessible is based purely on your actual business performance.

  • How is DMC different from other funding options?

    Unlike venture capital,DMC offers founders the opportunity to raise crucial growth capital without giving up control of their business. Equity can be one of the most expensive ways of raising working capital, but many founders are also wary of subjecting their businesses to the interest rates of traditional debt.DMC offers a fair and flexible alternative that doesn’t currently exist in the Global market.

  • Is DMC a substitute for Venture Capital?

    DMC works alongside or instead of venture capital and was established to address the fact that, for many small SaaS businesses, venture capital is simply not the best way to raise finance. DMC offers fair and flexible funding that can be used to finance a growing business without the founders having to give up equity or take on the burden of traditional debt. .
