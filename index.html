SME CREDIT GAP

World Bank group arm IFC reported 40 percent of formal micro, small and medium businesses in developed and developing countries, have an unmet financing need of $5.2 trillion every year.

Micro-, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs)are widely recognized for the important contributions they make to sustainable development, in terms of contributions to economic growth, creation of decent jobs, provision of public goods and services,as well as poverty alleviation and reduced inequality.

Short term cash flow constraints and limited access to finance is almost a universal challenge for MSMEs.In the short term,the lack of liquidity can leave enterprises unable to pay workers and suppliers or cover overheads and to forward plan and achieve scale in the medium to long run.

Green Credit card

DigitalMissionCapital is launch of Corporate Credit Card for SMEs/MSMEs and startup, called the Green Credit Card. It would give the SME’s and startups the freedom to avail credit facility for immediate needs and to manage their financial liquidity.

DigitalMissionCapital Green Credit Card offers customized billing cycle based on the repayment ability of the businesses.The company has come up with a Green Credit card to cater the varied billing cycles as per the working capital cycles of different businesses

Green Credit Card is designed to fulfil the credit requirements of SMEs & StartUps segment, which are highly underserved at present.

DMC App

SDG spending and Rewarding Application.

The Green credit card directly connects SMB's consumption to the impact it has on the planet, in a bid to encourage us to actively reduce our carbon footprint each day..

Small Medium Business can make their daily purchases with the Green Credit card, tracking the carbon emissions associated with their spending via the DMC app.

The app uses a calculation system measure the CO2 produced with every transaction, based on the analysis system provide catalytic finance and rewards to SMB's on the climate impact of their spending.
